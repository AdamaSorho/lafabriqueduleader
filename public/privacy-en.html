Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

We respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it for The Leader’s Inner Forge.

Data we collect

  • Email address and language to send the excerpt and follow up on pre-orders.
  • Name, phone (optional), country (optional), quantity and format for pre-orders.
  • Technical logs (IP address, anti-bot tokens) to protect the service.

Purpose

  • Send a transactional download link for the excerpt.
  • Process your pre-order request and contact you back.
  • Security: prevent abuse, rate limit, and manage bounces/complaints.

Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for these purposes, then delete or anonymize it.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We use service providers for hosting and email (Amazon Web Services). They process data on our behalf.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing contact@lafabriqueduleader.com.

Contact

For any questions, contact us at contact@lafabriqueduleader.com.
